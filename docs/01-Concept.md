# Concept

## Application Idea: Social Recipe App

The **Social Recipe Application** allows users to **create, share, and view recipes**.
In addition, users can **vote for and comment** on other users' recipes.
As a varied and healthy diet is important for good health, Social Recipe helps users **find and explore new ideas**.
Public recipes are at the heart of the Social Recipe application, but there is also support for **Private Recipes**, so Grandma's all-time-loved apple pie recipe can stay secret.
